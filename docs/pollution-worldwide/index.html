Exploring Air Pollution Globally


We’ll start by exploring global patterns in air pollution PM2.5 and social inequality.

The polluted air is not diffussed equally across the globe -quite the contrary. Some areas are exposed to much higher levels of air pollution than others (ref), and unfortunatly, these areas are not necessasrily populated with the most ressourcefull citizens. In this part of the work, we set out to explore the consequences of the unequal prevalence of air pollution.

In this part of the website we examine the diffussion of air pollution worldwide over time, and how this has harsh consequences for people living in less ressourcefull areas
